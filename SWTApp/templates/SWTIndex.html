CATCH TROUT.
WITH FLIES.
IN A LAKE.

All the information on this site is geared towards understanding fly fishing for trout in a lake. The insect imitations, the tackle and the techniques that work in a river don't necessarily apply when fly fishing stillwater. Everything here is for lake application.

If you're new, learn it all here.

Every day the lake offers up a new scenario. When you start out it's easy to have a good day on an exceptional day because you're not technically prepared. The truth is you can go on for ages doing something that doesn't work without even knowing. Some day you find out and fix it in seconds. This site is a place to get all the fundamentals down and eliminate any holes in your game.

If you're experienced this site is for sharing.

Part of fly fishing is bringing new people into the sport. Along with your helpful tips this link can be passed to fishing companions who are learning the ropes. The articles are carefully written and illustrated so picking up the ideas should be easy. The site goes beyond the basics and you could find insights into mysteries you're working on in your own fishing.
